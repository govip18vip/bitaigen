Binance Auto-Invest is criminally underrated. It's the simplest way to build a crypto portfolio without thinking about market timing or emotions. Let me show you how to set it up.

## What Is Dollar-Cost Averaging?

Instead of buying $1,000 of Bitcoin once, you buy $100 every week for 10 weeks. Both scenarios put $1,000 in, but DCA:

- Reduces risk from buying at the top
- Removes emotion from timing
- Smooths out volatility
- Is boring and predictable (which is good)

Professional investors use this. Binance Auto-Invest makes it automatic.

## Setting Up Auto-Invest

**On Desktop**:

1. Go to Binance.com
2. Click "Trade" → "Auto-Invest"
3. Click "Create Plan"
4. You'll see a form for:
   - Which crypto to invest in
   - How much per investment
   - How often (daily, weekly, monthly)
   - Start date
5. Fill it out
6. Click "Create" or "Confirm"

Done. Your plan is live.

**On Mobile**:

1. Open Binance app
2. Click "Buy" (bottom menu)
3. Look for "Auto-Invest" or similar
4. Click "Create Plan"
5. Same form as above
6. Create

The interface walks you through it.

## Step-By-Step Example

Let's create a plan to buy $100 of Bitcoin every week:

1. Click "Create Plan"
2. Select cryptocurrency: Bitcoin (BTC)
3. Enter amount: $100 USD (or your local currency)
4. Select frequency: Weekly
5. Select day: Monday
6. Start date: This week or next week
7. Review the plan
8. Click "Create"

That's it. Every Monday, Binance automatically buys $100 of Bitcoin using your account balance.

## Choosing Your Frequency

**Daily**: Invest every day. Most aggressive, smooths out volatility the best. Only good if you deposit daily.

**Weekly**: Most popular. Matches paycheck schedules well. Good balance.

**Monthly**: Less frequent. Good if you deposit monthly or want less overhead.

**Quarterly**: Very low frequency. Only use if you prefer rare, large purchases.

Most people use weekly.

## Choosing Your Amount

Start reasonable:

- **$50/week**: If you're new, small-risk testing
- **$100-200/week**: Standard beginner amount
- **$500+/week**: Larger commitment, requires steady income
- **$1,000+/month**: Serious investment position

Match it to your income and risk tolerance.

## Multiple Plans

You can create several Auto-Invest plans:

- $100/week Bitcoin
- $50/week Ethereum
- $50/week Polygon

Binance executes each separately. You can have as many as you want.

This is useful for portfolio diversification.

## How Auto-Invest Executes

On investment day (let's say Monday at 8am UTC):

1. Binance checks your account balance
2. Looks at current crypto price
3. Buys the exact amount at market rate
4. Credits to your spot wallet
5. You get a notification

The whole process takes seconds. You might see slight slippage (price moved between decision and execution), but it's minimal.

## What Happens If You Don't Have Funds?

If your account doesn't have enough balance on investment day, Auto-Invest skips that day.

**Example**: You set $100/week Bitcoin, but only have $50 in account on investment day. Binance skips, no error.

You need to maintain sufficient balance. Set up deposits before your investment dates.

## Best Practice: Match Your Income

If you get paid every Friday:

- Set Auto-Invest for Friday evening or Saturday
- Deposit your paycheck Friday morning
- Auto-Invest executes using fresh funds
- Repeat

This removes timing decisions entirely.

## Pausing or Editing Plans

If you need to pause (temporary cash crunch):

1. Go to Auto-Invest
2. Find the plan
3. Click "Pause"
4. It stops executing
5. Click "Resume" to restart

Editing a plan:

1. Go to the plan
2. Click "Edit"
3. Change amount, frequency, or crypto
4. Save
5. Changes take effect next cycle

## Emotionless Investing

The real power of Auto-Invest is removing emotion:

**Without Auto-Invest**: You watch Bitcoin price, wait for dips, panic when it drops, FOMO when it rises. You buy and sell erratically.

**With Auto-Invest**: You don't watch price. Every week, $100 goes in regardless. You build position automatically over time.

Over years, this is more profitable than trying to time the market.

## Tax Considerations

Each Auto-Invest purchase is a taxable event:

- Buying $100 Bitcoin counts as a $100 purchase
- Selling it later counts as a capital gain/loss

Keep records of all purchases (date, amount, price). Binance provides purchase history for tax purposes.

Consult a tax professional for your jurisdiction.

## Common Mistakes

Don't set Auto-Invest too high. If you can't afford it consistently, you'll have to pause, defeating the purpose.

Don't chase the latest hype coin. Auto-Invest is for boring, stable coins (Bitcoin, Ethereum, major altcoins). Use it for long-term building, not day-trading.

Don't forget to fund your account. Auto-Invest can't execute without balance.

## Auto-Invest vs. Trading

**Auto-Invest**: Set and forget, DCA, long-term, passive

**Trading**: Active, frequent, market timing, takes work

For most beginners, Auto-Invest is better. You're not trying to time markets. You're building position.
