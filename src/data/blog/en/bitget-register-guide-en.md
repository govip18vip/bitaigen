Bitget is gaining popularity, especially for copy trading (automatically copying other traders). Registration is fast and the interface is modern.

## Step 1: Create Account

Go to bitget.com and click "Sign Up."

Options:
- Email address
- Phone number
- Google account
- Apple ID

Email is standard. Enter email and password.

Verify your email (click the link), and registration is complete.

## Step 2: Set Up Security

Enable 2FA immediately:

1. Go to Account → Security
2. Find Two-Factor Authentication
3. Choose Authenticator App (recommended) or SMS

**Authenticator**:
- Download Google Authenticator or Authy
- Scan QR code
- Enter 6-digit code
- Save backup codes
- Confirm

This takes 2 minutes and secures your account.

## Step 3: Complete Identity Verification

You need KYC for fiat, but can trade crypto without it.

1. Go to Account → Verification
2. Select your country
3. Choose ID type
4. Take clear photo of ID
5. Take selfie
6. Submit

Standard KYC. Takes 10-15 minutes.

## Step 4: Fund Your Account

**With crypto** (fastest):
1. Go to Wallet → Deposit
2. Select cryptocurrency
3. Copy deposit address
4. Send from another exchange
5. Arrives in minutes

**With fiat** (after KYC):
1. Go to Deposit
2. Choose credit card or bank transfer
3. Enter amount
4. Complete payment
5. Crypto arrives

Credit card is instant (2-3% fee). Bank transfer takes 1-3 days (lower fees).

## Step 5: Your First Trade

Bitget has multiple trading modes. For beginners, use Spot:

1. Go to Spot Trading
2. Select trading pair (e.g., BTC/USDT)
3. Enter amount
4. Click Buy or place limit order
5. Confirm

Simple and straightforward.

## Copy Trading (Bitget's Main Feature)

Bitget's killer feature is copy trading:

1. Go to Copy Trading
2. Browse available traders
3. See their statistics (win rate, returns, risk)
4. Click Follow
5. Enter amount to copy
6. Their trades automatically execute in your account

This is perfect for beginners who don't know how to trade. You copy people who do.

**Risks**: If the trader you copy loses money, you lose too. Choose traders with good track records.

## Security Settings

**Master password**:
- Account → Security
- Create master password
- Required for sensitive changes

**Withdrawal whitelist**:
- Account → Security
- Add trusted addresses
- Can only withdraw to whitelisted

**IP whitelist**:
- Account → Security
- Restrict login to your IP

## Bitget's Features

**Spot Trading**: Buy and hold cryptos, simple.

**Futures/Perpetuals**: Leverage trading with contracts.

**Copy Trading**: Copy other traders automatically.

**Earn**: Staking and savings products.

Copy trading is where Bitget shines for beginners.

## Fees on Bitget

**Spot trading**: 0.1% maker, 0.15% taker

**Futures**: 0.02% maker, 0.055% taker

**Copy trading**: Fee shared between platform and trader (varies)

Competitive with other exchanges.

## Understanding the Interface

- **Markets**: All trading pairs and prices
- **Spot**: Buy/sell directly
- **Copy**: Follow other traders
- **Futures**: Advanced trading
- **Earn**: Passive income products
- **Wallet**: Manage funds

Everything is clearly labeled. Intuitive for beginners.

## Deposits and Withdrawals

**Deposit methods**:
- Credit card (instant, 2-3% fee)
- Bank transfer (1-3 days, 0-2% fee)
- Crypto (instant, free)

**Withdrawal speeds**:
- Crypto: 5-30 minutes
- Fiat: 1-3 days

**Withdrawal limits**: Depends on your KYC level. Complete KYC for higher limits.

## Why Choose Bitget?

**Copy trading**: If you don't want to trade yourself, follow successful traders.

**User-friendly**: Modern interface, easy to navigate.

**Growing**: Fast-growing exchange with good support.

**Competitive fees**: Similar to other major exchanges.

## Common Issues

**Verification slow**: Usually instant, but sometimes takes a few hours. Check email for updates.

**Can't deposit fiat**: You need full KYC. Complete verification first.

**Copy trading losses**: The trader you copy can lose money. Choose carefully and start small.

## After Registration

Start with small amounts ($50-200). If you're doing copy trading, pick traders with:
- Consistent returns (not one big win)
- Reasonable risk levels
- Several months of trading history

Beginners should avoid high-leverage copy traders.

- **vs Binance**: Binance is larger. Bitget has better copy trading.
- **vs Bybit**: Bybit better for futures. Bitget better for beginners and copy trading.
- **vs OKX**: Both are good. OKX more features, Bitget simpler.

Choose based on what you want to do.
