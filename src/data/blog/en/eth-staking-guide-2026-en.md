Earn 5-7% APY on Ethereum. Staking is one of the simplest ways to earn passive income in crypto.

## What is Staking?

Staking locks your cryptocurrency in a blockchain network to support its operations, earning rewards in return (similar to bank interest).

## How to Get Started

### Option 1: Exchange Staking (Easiest)

1. On Binance or OKX, go to [Sign up for Binance (code B2345)](/go/binance)
2. Navigate to "Earn" or "Staking" section
3. Select asset and duration
4. Confirm staking



> [Sign up for Binance (code B2345)](/go/binance) | [Sign up for OKX (code B2345)](/go/okx)


### Option 2: On-Chain Staking

Stake directly on-chain for higher yields but more complex operation.

| Platform | APY | Lock Period |

## Risk Warning

- Staked assets are locked and may not be immediately sellable
- Yields fluctuate with market conditions
- Smart contract risks exist

## FAQ

### Is staking risky?

Main risks are price volatility during lock period and smart contract vulnerabilities. Using major exchanges reduces contract risk.

### What is the minimum staking amount?

Most exchanges support very low minimums, typically starting from a few dollars.
