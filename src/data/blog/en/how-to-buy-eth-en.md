Ethereum (ticker: **ETH**) is the world's second-largest cryptocurrency by market cap and the leading smart contract platform powering DeFi, NFTs, and Web3 applications. Here's how to buy your first ETH in four simple steps.

> ⚠️ Risk warning: Cryptocurrency prices are highly volatile. Never invest more than you can afford to lose.

## Table of contents

## What you need before you start

- A valid email address or phone number
- Government-issued ID (passport or national ID) for KYC
- A payment method (bank transfer, debit/credit card)

## Step 1 — Create a Binance account

1. Go to [www.binance.com](https://www.binance.com)
2. Click **Register** in the top-right corner
3. Enter your email address and create a strong password
4. Verify your email via the confirmation link
5. Enable **two-factor authentication (2FA)** using Google Authenticator — this is critical for account security

## Step 2 — Complete KYC verification

KYC (Know Your Customer) is mandatory to unlock fiat purchase features.

1. Click your profile icon → **Identification**
2. Select your country of residence
3. Upload a clear photo of your **government-issued ID** (front and back)
4. Complete **facial recognition** (liveness check)
5. Wait for approval — usually under 10 minutes

## Step 3 — Fund your account

Once verified, deposit funds:

1. Click **Buy Crypto** → **Bank Deposit** (lowest fees) or **Credit/Debit Card** (instant)
2. Select your local currency (USD, EUR, GBP, etc.)
3. Enter the amount and follow the payment instructions
4. Funds typically arrive within minutes for card payments, or 1–3 business days for bank transfers

## Step 4 — Buy ETH

With funds ready, place your order:

1. Click **Trade** → **Spot** in the top menu
2. Search for **ETH/USDT**
3. Select **Market** order type (executes immediately at current price)
4. Enter the amount you want to spend
5. Click **Buy ETH** and confirm

Your ETH will appear in your **Spot Wallet** instantly.

## Where to store your ETH

| Storage method | Security | Best for |
|---------------|----------|---------|
| Exchange wallet | ⭐⭐⭐ | Active trading, small amounts |
| MetaMask (software wallet) | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ | DeFi, regular use |
| Ledger (hardware wallet) | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Long-term holding, large amounts |

> Your seed phrase (recovery phrase) is the only backup for your wallet. **Write it on paper, store it offline, never photograph or cloud-sync it.**

## Summary

Buying ETH takes four steps: sign up → verify identity → fund account → place order. The whole process takes under 30 minutes. Start small to get comfortable, then scale up as you gain confidence.
